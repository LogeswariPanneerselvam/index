AI for Sustainable Development by Game Design

A symposium on integrating AI with sustainable development through game design

Welcome to AI Fusion 2024

Event Overview

Objective: Integrating AI in sustainable development within the context of a game design competition can create a unique and impactful experience. This will exhibit and depict the creativity, problem solving, collaboration, communication, and prototyping skills of participants.

Outcome Expected: Encourage participants to develop innovative gameplay mechanics that integrate AI, teaching players about sustainability, and to generate and implement creative AI-driven solutions for sustainable development.

Event Phases

Phase 1: Learning

From August 21 to 27

  • Introduction to Game Design and SDG
  • Introduction to Unity3D
  • Unity3D Scripting
  • Fundamentals of 3D Modeling
  • AR / VR integration in Unity3D

Phase 2: Build and Compete

On August 28 and 29 at campus

Schedule

Date & Time: Registration from August 5 to 20, 2024. Event: August 28 & 29, 2024

Location: Vel Tech Rangarajan Dr. Sagunthala R&D Institute of Science and Technology, Avadi, Chennai.

Activities

Day 1 (August 28, 2024)

An Overview of AI Usage in Gaming and AR/VR Technologies: Implementation and Execution.

Day 2 (August 29, 2024)

The buildathon involves implementing AI in innovative gameplay mechanics, focusing on a specific sustainability development theme. There will be an award ceremony to recognize the best projects.
