AI for Sustainable Development by Game Design

Integrating AI in sustainable development within the context of a game design competition can create a unique and impactful experience. This will exhibit and depict the creativity, problem-solving, collaboration, communication, and prototyping skills of participants.

Outcome Expected: Encourage participants to develop innovative gameplay mechanics that integrate AI, teaching players about sustainability, and to generate and implement creative AI-driven solutions for sustainable development. The event involves two phases:

Phase 1: Learning (from August 21 to 27)
In this phase, the participants will be given training on the Unity environment. Experts from ARK will be leading the instructor-led live sessions on the following topics. Each participant will be provided with resources to do Self-Paced Online Training (SPOT):

  • Introduction to Game Design and SDG
  • Introduction to Unity3D
  • Unity3D Scripting
  • Fundamentals of 3D Modeling
  • AR / VR integration in Unity3D

Phase 2: Build and Compete (on August 28 and 29 at campus)
Day 1 (28th Aug 2024) - An Overview of AI Usage in Gaming and AR/VR Technologies: Implementation and Execution.
Day 2 (29th Aug 2024) - The buildathon involves implementing AI in innovative gameplay mechanics, focusing on a specific sustainability development theme. There will be an award ceremony to recognize the best projects.
